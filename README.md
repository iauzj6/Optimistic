# VMISS how about it? VMISS introduction and review

## VMISS Introduction

VMISS (Virtual Machine Innovative Solutions) is a Canadian technology and IT infrastructure company based in Toronto, Ontario, established in 2021. The company offers high-performance cloud servers, hosting, and compute services worldwide from a network of global locations. VMISS specializes in providing VPS solutions with optimized network connectivity, particularly focusing on China-optimized routes including CN2 GIA, BGP, and intelligent routing technologies.

The company recently launched its JP.TKY.TRI series VPS packages featuring tri-network direct connection optimization (4134 + 4837 + 58453) with intelligent routing functionality. For website hosting scenarios, China Telecom return traffic automatically prioritizes CN2 GIA routes, providing faster and more stable connections. VMISS operates multiple data centers across Hong Kong, Japan (Tokyo and Osaka), South Korea (Seoul), United States (Los Angeles), and the United Kingdom (London).
